The Costs & Benefits of OAA Funding

Title III of the Older Americans Act of 1965 (OAA) enables states to fund programs that support aging in place, such as home delivered meals, homemaker services, case management, transportation, and community-based meals. Studies suggest that state investments in such community-based service programs have proportionally fewer low-care nursing home residents.
